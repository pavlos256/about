/about/

Software and Web Developer

Having over 16 years of professional experience in software development, I've worked with many different technologies on a wide range of software solutions by myself or as part of a team. I pay attention to detail and I strive to deliver the best possible result. I pride myself on being very honest and straight forward.

I'm experienced in multithreaded applications, networking, databases, services, client/server, web applications, security and many aspects of game programming. My background in early PCs and low-level programming has given me a thorough understanding of computing and helps me identify possible performance bottlenecks and overcome them.

I keep up to date with the latest developments and I believe in using the right tool for the job. Having started as a DOS/Windows developer with C/C++ and some Visual Basic, I now use several languages on both Windows and Linux depending on the project requirements.

— Programming is more than a job; it's an expression of creativity and it can be art.
